Dr. Md Rushdie Ibne Islam is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mechanics at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi since June 2022. Prior to joining IIT Delhi, Dr. Islam worked as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani. He was in the Faculty of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Technion-IIT Israel and in the Department of Biomedical Engineering and Mechanics at Virginia Tech., USA during his postdoctoral studies. He also worked as an Senior Researcher in ESS Engineering Software Steyr GmbH, Austria. He received Ph.D. in Computational Mechanics and M.Tech. in Structural Engineering from the Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. He received his B.E. in Civil Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata.

His current research interests include fracture mechanics, computational damage mechanics, impact mechanics, particle-based methods, fluid-structure interaction, molecular dynamics and multi-scale modelling.
