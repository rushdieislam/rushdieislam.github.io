Welcome to the CoMPAS Laboratory at IIT Delhi!

Computational Mechanics for Prognosis, Analysis, and Design of Engineering Systems

The CoMPAS lab, led by Dr. Ritwik Bandyopadhyay from the Applied Mechanics Department at IIT Delhi, specializes broadly in computational solid mechanics. We utilize tools such as the finite element method to solve practical problems across the mechanical sciences. While our primary focus is on computation, we also engage in experimental work and collaborate with experts in this field. Currently, our research is concentrated on three areas: microstructure-sensitive fatigue modeling, multi-scale modeling of residual stress, and uncertainty quantification and propagation. The CoMPAS lab welcomes collaborations based on mutual interests and complementary expertise. Please visit our research page and contact our group leader for more information.
